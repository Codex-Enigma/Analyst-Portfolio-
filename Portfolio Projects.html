Netflix Content Analysis

This project explores the Netflix dataset to uncover insights about its content library, trends, and patterns. It includes data cleaning, visualization, and analysis using Python libraries like pandas, matplotlib, seaborn, and wordcloud. The analysis revealed that drama, love, and family genres have seen a significant spike, with TV shows now outnumbering movies, which average around 90 minutes. Content from the USA and India dominate the platform, with over 50% of the content coming from these countries. The findings also highlighted key patterns in release years and ratings, providing actionable insights into content curation and audience targeting.
